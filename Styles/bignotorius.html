Limited silver colored vinyl. The Notorious B.I.G.'s album 'Life After Death' stands as a monumental masterpiece in hip-hop history. Released in 1997, shortly after the artist's tragic passing, the album is a testament to his immense talent and lyrical prowess. Spanning two discs, it showcases Biggie's storytelling abilities, impeccable flow, and charismatic presence. From the gritty street narratives of "Mo Money Mo Problems" and "Hypnotize" to the introspective tracks like "Sky's The Limit" and "Miss U," the album traverses themes of fame, success, and the harsh realities of life. 'Life After Death' features notable collaborations with artists like Jay-Z, R. Kelly, and Lil' Kim, further solidifying its status as a classic in the genre.
