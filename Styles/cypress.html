CYPRESS HILL Title BLACK SUNDAY Black Sunday is the second studio album by American hip hop group Cypress Hill, originally released on July 20, 1993 by Ruffhouse and Columbia Records. The album went Triple platinum in the U.S., with 3.4 million units sold. All tracks produced by DJ Muggs, except "I Ain't Goin' Out Like That" produced by T-Ray.
